Pork Adobo Recipe

Famous Filipino Dish

Adobo is one of the well-known gateway dishes in the Philippines. It involves simmering meat and even seafood in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds pork meat (cut into chunks)
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 6 cloves garlic (choped)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 teaspoons black peppercorns
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. In a meduim dutch oven or pot over meduim high heat, add the oil and sear the pork until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the vinegar, soy sauce, garlic bay leaf, peppercorns, sugar, and water, and bring to a boil.
    Reduce the heat to meduim low, cover, and simmer for 1 hour.
  3. Remove the cover and continue simmering for another 30 minutes to reduce the sauce
  4. serve over rice
